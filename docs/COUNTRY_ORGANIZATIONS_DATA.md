# Country Organizations Data

Comprehensive list of international organizations, their members, and data for the app's Organization feature.

> **Currently implemented** in `Organization.swift`: UN, NATO, EU, ASEAN, AU (African Union), Arab League, BRICS, CIS, Commonwealth, G7, G20, Mercosur, OECD, OPEC, SCO, USMCA, WTO

---

## Organizations Currently in App

### United Nations (UN)
- **Founded**: 1945
- **HQ**: New York, USA
- **Members**: 193 member states + 2 observer states (Palestine, Vatican City)
- **Missing from 193**: Kosovo, Taiwan, Western Sahara (SADR), etc.
- **All 193 member states** are in the UN — essentially every country in `countries.json` except Kosovo, Taiwan, and a few others

### NATO (North Atlantic Treaty Organization)
- **Founded**: 1949
- **HQ**: Brussels, Belgium
- **Members (32 as of 2024)**:
  Albania, Belgium, Bulgaria, Canada, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Montenegro, Netherlands, North Macedonia, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States

### European Union (EU)
- **Founded**: 1993 (Maastricht Treaty; predecessor EEC 1957)
- **HQ**: Brussels, Belgium (also Strasbourg for Parliament)
- **Members (27)**:
  Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden
- **Note**: UK left (Brexit) 2020. Candidate countries: Ukraine, Moldova, Georgia, Albania, North Macedonia, Montenegro, Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kosovo

### ASEAN (Association of Southeast Asian Nations)
- **Founded**: 1967
- **HQ**: Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Members (10)**:
  Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam
- **Observer**: Timor-Leste (candidate for membership)

### African Union (AU)
- **Founded**: 2002 (replaced OAU, 1963)
- **HQ**: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
- **Members**: 55 member states — all African countries including Western Sahara (SADR)
- **Full list**: Algeria, Angola, Benin, Botswana, Burkina Faso, Burundi, Cameroon, Cape Verde, Central African Republic, Chad, Comoros, DRC, Republic of Congo, Djibouti, Egypt, Equatorial Guinea, Eritrea, Eswatini, Ethiopia, Gabon, Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Lesotho, Liberia, Libya, Madagascar, Malawi, Mali, Mauritania, Mauritius, Morocco (rejoined 2017), Mozambique, Namibia, Niger, Nigeria, Rwanda, São Tomé and Príncipe, Senegal, Seychelles, Sierra Leone, Somalia, South Africa, South Sudan, Sudan, Tanzania, Togo, Tunisia, Uganda, Western Sahara (SADR), Zambia, Zimbabwe

### Arab League (League of Arab States)
- **Founded**: 1945
- **HQ**: Cairo, Egypt
- **Members (22)**:
  Algeria, Bahrain, Comoros, Djibouti, Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Libya, Mauritania, Morocco, Oman, Palestine, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Somalia, Sudan, Syria (suspended 2011–2023, readmitted 2023), Tunisia, UAE, Yemen

### BRICS
- **Founded**: 2009 (first summit; acronym coined 2001)
- **HQ**: No permanent HQ; rotating presidency
- **Original 5**: Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa
- **2024 expansion (joined Jan 2024)**: Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, UAE
- **Invited but declined/deferred**: Argentina (declined under Milei), Saudi Arabia (deferred), others
- **BRICS+ invited (2023)**: Many more countries expressed interest

### CIS (Commonwealth of Independent States)
- **Founded**: 1991
- **HQ**: Minsk, Belarus
- **Members (9 active)**:
  Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Moldova, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan
- **Associate**: Turkmenistan
- **Formerly**: Georgia (withdrew 2009), Ukraine (de facto suspended since 2018)

### Commonwealth of Nations
- **Founded**: 1931 (Statute of Westminster); current form 1949
- **HQ**: London, UK
- **Members (56)**:
  Antigua and Barbuda, Australia, Bahamas, Bangladesh, Barbados, Belize, Botswana, Brunei, Cameroon, Canada, Cyprus, Dominica, Eswatini, Fiji, Gambia, Ghana, Grenada, Guyana, India, Jamaica, Kenya, Kiribati, Lesotho, Malawi, Malaysia, Maldives, Malta, Mauritius, Mozambique, Namibia, Nauru, New Zealand, Nigeria, Pakistan, Papua New Guinea, Rwanda, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Saint Lucia, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, Samoa, Seychelles, Sierra Leone, Singapore, Solomon Islands, South Africa, Sri Lanka, Tanzania, Tonga, Trinidad and Tobago, Tuvalu, Uganda, United Kingdom, Vanuatu, Zambia
- **Recently**: Togo and Gabon joined (2022); Zimbabwe rejoined (2018)

### G7 (Group of Seven)
- **Founded**: 1975 (G6), 1976 (G7)
- **Members**: Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, United Kingdom, United States
- **Also**: European Union participates but is not a formal member
- **Russia**: Was G8 member 2002–2014, expelled after Crimea annexation

### G20 (Group of Twenty)
- **Founded**: 1999
- **Members (19 countries + 2 unions)**:
  Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, South Korea, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States + EU + AU (added 2023)

### Mercosur
- **Founded**: 1991 (Treaty of Asunción)
- **HQ**: Montevideo, Uruguay
- **Full members**: Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay
- **Suspended**: Venezuela (since 2016, democracy clause)
- **Bolivia**: In process of full accession
- **Associate members**: Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Guyana, Peru, Suriname
- **Observer**: New Zealand, Mexico

### OECD (Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development)
- **Founded**: 1961
- **HQ**: Paris, France
- **Members (38)**:
  Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Mexico, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Slovakia, Slovenia, South Korea, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States
- **Accession in progress**: Argentina, Brazil, Bulgaria, Croatia, Indonesia, Peru, Romania, Saudi Arabia

### OPEC (Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries)
- **Founded**: 1960
- **HQ**: Vienna, Austria
- **Members (12)**:
  Algeria, Congo (Rep.), Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Iran, Iraq, Kuwait, Libya, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Venezuela
- **Former members**: Ecuador (left 2020), Indonesia (suspended), Qatar (left 2019)
- **OPEC+**: OPEC + 10 non-members (Russia, Mexico, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Brunei, Malaysia, Oman, South Sudan, Sudan)

### SCO (Shanghai Cooperation Organisation)
- **Founded**: 2001
- **HQ**: Beijing, China
- **Members (9 as of 2023)**:
  China, India, Iran (2023), Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan
- **Dialogue partners**: Armenia, Azerbaijan, Cambodia, Egypt, Nepal, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Turkey, UAE

### USMCA (United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement)
- **In force**: 2020 (replaced NAFTA)
- **Members**: United States, Mexico, Canada

### WTO (World Trade Organization)
- **Founded**: 1995 (replaced GATT)
- **HQ**: Geneva, Switzerland
- **Members**: 164 member states (most of the world)
- **Observer**: ~25 countries (including Iran, Iraq, Libya, South Sudan, etc.)

  Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka

- **Founded**: 2000 (revived; original 1967–1977)
- **HQ**: Arusha, Tanzania
- **Members (8)**:
  Burundi, DRC, Kenya, Rwanda, Somalia, South Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda

### SADC (Southern African Development Community)
- **Founded**: 1992
- **HQ**: Gaborone, Botswana
- **Members (16)**:
  Angola, Botswana, Comoros, DRC, Eswatini, Lesotho, Madagascar, Malawi, Mauritius, Mozambique, Namibia, Seychelles, South Africa, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe

### Nordic Council
- **Founded**: 1952
- **Members**: Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, Sweden (+ autonomous territories: Faroe Islands, Greenland, Åland)

### Visegrád Group (V4)
- **Founded**: 1991
- **Members**: Czech Republic, Hungary, Poland, Slovakia

### CELAC (Community of Latin American and Caribbean States)
- **Founded**: 2011
- **Members**: 33 Latin American and Caribbean states (essentially all except USA, Canada)

### OAS (Organization of American States)
- **Founded**: 1948
- **HQ**: Washington D.C., USA
- **Members**: 35 (all independent states of the Americas; Cuba was suspended 1962–2009)

## Data Enhancement for `countries.json`

Currently `countries.json` likely stores organization IDs as strings per country. The full membership data should be cross-referenced to allow:
1. "Members of X organization" query (show all members on a map / list)
2. "Which organizations does Country X belong to" (country detail)
3. Organization quiz questions ("Which organization does Iceland belong to?" → NATO, UN)

The `Organization.all` static array in the app is the right place to add member country code arrays:
```swift
struct Organization {
    // ...
    let memberCodes: [String]  // ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 codes
}
```
